Here is the structured, polished roadmap for your **Lean Storytelling App**, organized by development phases and core objectives.

---

## 🚀 Product Vision
An application designed for leaders and managers to master **Lean Storytelling**. It transitions from a simple structuring tool to an AI-powered coaching platform that facilitates high-stakes influence and compelling communication.

---

## 🏗️ Development Roadmap

### Phase 1: The "Vanilla" Foundation (V1)
*Focus: Validation of core logic, UI/UX, and local functionality.*
* **Tech Stack:** Vanilla JS, CSS, HTML (Local-first, no cloud deployment).
* **Core Feature:** A simple form to capture the "Minimum Viable Story."
* **Data Inputs:** Target, Problem, and Solution.
* **Output:** Visualization of the basic building blocks.
* **Success Metric:** Is the design neat, clean, and does the basic logic hold up?

### Phase 2: Methodological Enrichment (V2)
*Focus: Transitioning from building blocks to a comprehensive narrative.*
* **Wave A (The Details):**
    * **Target:** Enriched with **Empathy** (Who are they really?).
    * **Problem:** Enriched with **Consequences** (What is at stake?).
    * **Solution:** Enriched with **Benefits** (The "What’s in it for them?").
* **Wave B (The Narrative Arc):**
    * **Context:** Setting the stage.
    * **Call to Action:** Ending with the "Why" and a clear question/ask.
* **Objective:** Match the depth of a physical workshop/canvas experience.

### Phase 3: Intelligent Coaching & Personalization (V3)
*Focus: Validating the "Risky Assumption"—do users want constant guidance?*
* **The Feature:** Integration of programmatic algorithms and/or **AI** to provide real-time advice and examples.
* **Personalization:** Tailoring coaching based on:
    * The user's profile and seniority.
    * Existing knowledge level.
    * Specific communication goals.
* **Key Question:** Is AI-driven coaching worth the cost/effort, or is programmatic guidance sufficient?

### Phase 4: Feedback Loops & Delivery (V4)
*Focus: Validating the story against the real world.*
* **External Validation:** Tools to gather feedback from human stakeholders.
* **Consistency Check:** Is the story well-architected, consistent, and convincing?
* **Iterative Refinement:** A workflow to input human feedback back into the app to generate the next version of the story.
