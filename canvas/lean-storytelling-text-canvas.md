# Lean Storytelling Canvas

## How to Use This Canvas

This canvas helps you structure your story using the Lean Storytelling methodology. Follow these steps:

1. **Copy this file** to create your own version (e.g., `my-story.md`)
2. **Fill in each section** with your specific information
3. **Be concise and specific** - focus on the most important details
4. **Review and refine** - ensure each section flows logically to the next
5. **Use your completed story** in presentations, documents, or conversations
